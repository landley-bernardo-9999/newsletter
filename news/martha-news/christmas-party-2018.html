Christmas Party 2018



The Martha as one of the the 3 subcompanies of Koord held their Christmas party at the Pine Breeze hotel last December 21, 2018. The celebration was full of activities, games, and surprises that were all meant to give back to what the employees have done in order make the 2018 a success one. The CEO himself, Atty. Alexander Bangsoy delivered an inspiring speech and highlighted the importance of leadership in an organization. He also shared the plans of the company for the 2019 focusing more on expansion and providing service to all to its clients, customers, and also to the community. At the end of the program, each employee received a Noche Buena package.
